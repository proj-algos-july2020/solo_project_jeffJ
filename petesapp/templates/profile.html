Workingout together for cause Com3unity

Coach Peter Tapao

My fitness journey started when I diagnosed with Acute Eczema and lived constant pain. I was miserable, I was picked on, people were scared of me, and hated every moment of my life growing up.
It was in the playground that I felt most free and dreamed of being an athlete. I worked hard and pushed harder to become more athletic and discovered my passion for fitness. Personal training and group exercise was my platform of stepping out of my shell. I want to show and teach people how to push, overcome, and be resilient by exercising your mind and body. I've broken through all my adversities through fitness and want to share these experiances so other we can brake through anything. Giving back and blessing people with this hope is what I strive for to make the world a better place.
Fitness is my lifestyle and welcome to Com3unity.

My Promise To You

I promise to motivate, encourage and push you to be your best. My goal is to prepare you to prepare you for your fitness journey. We will work together to execute, meet, and exceed your goals, and find the new you.
I'm here for your progress in this journey. I'm here to be your coach!
